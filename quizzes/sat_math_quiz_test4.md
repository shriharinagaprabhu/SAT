## 🔑 Answer Key & Explanations

> [!CAUTION]
> Don't peek until you've attempted all 15 questions!

### Q1: **D) 8x + 3 = 83**
**Domain:** Algebra | **Skill:** Translating words → equations
"8 times a number x" = 8x. "3 more than" that = 8x + 3. Set equal to 83: **8x + 3 = 83**.

---

### Q2: **A) With each monthly deposit, the amount increased by $25.**
**Domain:** Algebra | **Skill:** Interpreting linear functions
In f(t) = 100 + 25t, the coefficient of *t* (which is 25) represents the rate of change — the amount added per deposit. The 100 is the initial amount (y-intercept).

---

### Q3: **A) 25%**
**Domain:** PSDA | **Skill:** Percentages
75/300 = 0.25 = **25%**.

---

### Q4: **A) 0**
**Domain:** Algebra | **Skill:** Solving linear equations with substitution
Substitute r = 16: 2.5b + 5(16) = 80 → 2.5b + 80 = 80 → 2.5b = 0 → **b = 0**.

---

### Q5: **y = 96**
**Domain:** Algebra | **Skill:** Systems of linear equations (elimination)
Subtract the second equation from the first:
(24x + y) − (6x + y) = 48 − 72 → 18x = −24 → x = −4/3.
Substitute into 6x + y = 72: 6(−4/3) + y = 72 → −8 + y = 72 → **y = 80**... 

Let me recheck: 24(−4/3) + y = 48 → −32 + y = 48 → y = 80.  
Check: 6(−4/3) + 80 = −8 + 80 = 72 ✅. **y = 80**.

---

### Q6: **D) y = −x/3 + 13**
**Domain:** Algebra | **Skill:** Point-slope form / slope-intercept
Using point-slope: y − 10 = −1/3(x − 9) → y − 10 = −x/3 + 3 → y = **−x/3 + 13**.

---

### Q7: **B) The value of the bank account is estimated to be approximately 243 dollars in 1962.**
**Domain:** Advanced Math | **Skill:** Interpreting exponential functions
f(5) means 5 years after 1957 = 1962. f(5) ≈ 243 means the account value was about **$243 in 1962**.

---

### Q8: **B) It must increase by 24.5 units.**
**Domain:** PSDA | **Skill:** Ratios and proportions
Ratio = 35/10 = 3.5. If width increases by 7, length must increase by 7 × 3.5 = **24.5 units**.

---

### Q9: **A) f(x) = (x + 44)²**
**Domain:** Advanced Math | **Skill:** Polynomial/function modeling
Perimeter of P = 4x. Perimeter of Q = 4x + 176. Side of Q = (4x + 176)/4 = x + 44. Area of Q = **(x + 44)²**.

---

### Q10: **100°**
**Domain:** Geometry & Trig | **Skill:** Central angles and arcs
A central angle equals the measure of its intercepted arc. Since O is the center and arc RS = 100°, angle ROS = **100°**.

---

### Q11: **B) 9/4**
**Domain:** Advanced Math | **Skill:** Solving quadratic equations
Rearrange: 4x² + 7x − 36 = 0. Using the quadratic formula:
x = (−7 ± √(49 + 576))/8 = (−7 ± √625)/8 = (−7 ± 25)/8.
Positive solution: (−7 + 25)/8 = 18/8 = **9/4**.

---

### Q12: **0.3 or 3/10**
**Domain:** PSDA | **Skill:** Probability from two-way tables
Total red tiles = 30. Total tiles = 100. P(red) = 30/100 = **0.3** (or 3/10).

---

### Q13: **B) 1/7**
**Domain:** Algebra | **Skill:** No-solution conditions for linear equations
−3x + 21px = 84 → x(−3 + 21p) = 84. For no solution, the coefficient of x must equal 0 while the constant ≠ 0:
−3 + 21p = 0 → p = 3/21 = **1/7**. (84 ≠ 0 ✅, so no solution.)

---

### Q14: **D) −3/2**
**Domain:** Advanced Math | **Skill:** Vertex of a quadratic (factored form)
The x-coordinate of the vertex is the average of the roots: (10 + (−13))/2 = −3/2. f(x) reaches its minimum at **x = −3/2**.

---

### Q15: **D) x² + (y + 1)² = 49**
**Domain:** Geometry & Trig | **Skill:** Circle equations and transformations
Circle A has center (0, 1). Shifting down 2 units → new center (0, −1). The equation becomes x² + (y − (−1))² = 49 → **x² + (y + 1)² = 49**.

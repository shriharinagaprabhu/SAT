## 2. Detailed Milestone Breakdown

### Junior Year (11th Grade): August 2026 – June 2027
*   **August–October 2026:** Jump straight into challenging coursework. GPA during the 11th grade is the most critical academic factor colleges review.
*   **October 2026:** Take the **PSAT/NMSQT**. Qualifying scores can lead to National Merit Scholarships.
*   **January–February 2027:** Begin rigorous prep for the **SAT/ACT**. Utilize official practice tests.
*   **March–April 2027:** Take your first official SAT/ACT attempt. Discuss college search goals and courses for senior year with the school counselor.
*   **May 2027:** Take AP or IB exams.
*   **Late May/June 2027:** Ask two academic teachers for letters of recommendation before high school lets out for the summer.

### Summer Break: June 2027 – August 2027
*   **June–July 2027:** Categorize college choices into Reach, Target, and Safety lists. Brainstorm and draft the main Common Application personal statement essay.
*   **August 1, 2027:** The **Common Application** opens. Create an account, fill out core profile details, and start drafting college-specific supplemental essays.

### Senior Year (12th Grade): August 2027 – June 2028
*   **September 2027:** Request high school transcripts from the registrar. Retake the SAT/ACT one final time if aiming for a higher score.
*   **October 2027:** Submit FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) and CSS Profile financial aid forms when they open.
*   **November 1 / 15, 2027:** Submit **Early Action (EA)** and **Early Decision (ED)** college applications.
*   **January 1 / 15, 2028:** Submit all remaining **Regular Decision (RD)** applications.
*   **March–April 2028:** Admissions decisions and financial aid offers are released.
*   **May 1, 2028:** **National Decision Day**. Submit an enrollment deposit to one chosen college.
*   **August–September 2028:** Move in and begin college!

## Quick Start

1. **Print** `sat-practice-test-4-digital.pdf` and take it under timed conditions in Week 1.
2. **Score** the test and record results in `score_tracker.md`.
3. **Identify** your 3 weakest topic areas.
4. **Follow** the Phase 1 plan in `phase1_foundation.md`.
5. **Check off** daily and weekly items using `weekly_checklist.md`.

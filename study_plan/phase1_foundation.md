# Phase 1: Diagnostic & Foundation (Weeks 1–4)
**Dates:** August 2026 | **Practice Test Used:** Test 4 (Diagnostic)

---

## Week 1: Diagnostic Test & Analysis

**Goal:** Establish a baseline score using Practice Test 4 and identify weak areas.

| Day | Time | Activity |
|-----|------|----------|
| **Mon** | 30 min | ☐ Set up study space. Print `sat-practice-test-4-digital.pdf`. Review SAT format (2 sections, 4 modules, 98 questions, 2 hr 14 min). |
| **Tue** | 30 min | ☐ Study the SAT content domains: RW (Craft & Structure, Information & Ideas, Standard English Conventions, Expression of Ideas) and Math (Algebra, Advanced Math, Problem Solving & Data Analysis, Geometry & Trig). |
| **Wed** | 15 min | ☐ Review test-day simulation rules: timed, no phone, quiet room, approved calculator only. Prepare materials for Saturday. |
| **Thu** | 30 min | ☐ Do a mini warm-up: 10 sample RW questions (untimed) to get comfortable with question styles. |
| **Fri** | 30 min | ☐ Do a mini warm-up: 10 sample Math questions (untimed) to get comfortable with question styles. |
| **Sat** | 3 hr | ☐ **TAKE PRACTICE TEST 4** under full timed conditions. RW: 64 min → Break: 10 min → Math: 70 min. |
| **Sun** | 1.5 hr | ☐ **SCORE & ANALYZE.** Record score in `score_tracker.md`. Categorize every wrong answer by topic and error type (C/K/T). List your top 5 weakest topics. |

## Week 2: Reading & Writing Foundations

**Goal:** Build foundational understanding of all four RW content domains.

| Day | Time | Activity |
|-----|------|----------|
| **Mon** | 70 min | ☐ **Craft & Structure** — Study: central ideas, themes, purpose, and text structure. Practice: 10 questions on identifying main ideas and author purpose. Log errors. |
| **Tue** | 70 min | ☐ **Information & Ideas** — Study: command of evidence, inferences, and data interpretation in passages. Practice: 10 questions on drawing conclusions from text. Log errors. |
| **Wed** | 70 min | ☐ **Standard English Conventions** — Study: subject-verb agreement, pronoun clarity, verb tense consistency. Practice: 10 grammar-focused questions. Log errors. |
| **Thu** | 70 min | ☐ **Expression of Ideas** — Study: transitions, logical flow, rhetorical synthesis. Practice: 10 questions on improving sentence clarity and transitions. Log errors. |
| **Fri** | 70 min | ☐ **Mixed RW Review** — Timed drill: 27 mixed RW questions in 32 min (simulate one module). Score and log all errors. |
| **Sat** | 2 hr | ☐ **Deep Review** — Re-do every wrong question from this week without a timer. Write out why the correct answer is right. Study 15 vocabulary-in-context words. |
| **Sun** | 30 min | ☐ **Reflect** — Fill out weekly checklist. Rate confidence on each RW topic 1–5. Set goals for Week 3. |
